# Spyro 3 Poptracker

# To Use

* Download [Poptracker](https://github.com/black-sliver/PopTracker/releases)
* Download the [latest release](https://github.com/riezahughes/MedievilAp-Poptracker/releases) of this pack
* Put the zip file into the "packs" folder of poptracker
* Run poptracker and load the pack
* Click the "ap" icon and connect to your AP game!

# Notes

Due to the nature of Spyro 3, almost anything can be accessed out of logic with various tricks.

To allow the tracker to still convey meaningful information, only the easiest of out of logic skips will be marked yellow.



In particular the following can be marked as accessible out of logic:

* All skateboarding and Hunter speedway eggs.  Hunter is captured for 2 levels in Evening Lake, meaning the checks cannot officially be in logic until after Scorch.  No tricks are required to access these checks.
* Entering Molten Crater's Sgt. Byrd area early, by jumping on the bridge posts and gliding on top of the hut.
* All of Spooky Swamp without the Moneybags door, which can be done with various damage boosts, out of bounds glides, or proxies.
* Entering Bamboo Terrace's Bentley area early, using a swim in air.  Go into the water by the waterfall at the end of level, get close to the edge, and tap square.
* Entering Fireworks Factory's Agent 9 area early, with a careful glide into the right "antenna" at the top of the hut.
